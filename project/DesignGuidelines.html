Design guidelines

Design guidelines is a set of architectural and urban guidelines and requirements that contribute to guiding the urban design and development process to ensure consistency with the authentic local architectural style of each geographic area across the Kingdom. These guidelines are applied to the design and development of buildings and public spaces.

Design Styles in Saudi Architecture

Each Saudi architectural style encompasses three main design approaches:

  1. Traditional Style: Reflects the authentic heritage of architecture, drawing its elements from local environments and traditional materials.
  2. Transitional Style: Blends traditional and contemporary elements in a way that preserves identity while keeping up with modern times.
  3. Contemporary Style: A modern design inspired by the principles of Saudi architecture but utilizing advanced techniques and approaches to meet modern needs.
